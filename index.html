NASA Sounding Rocket Launches
into Alaskan Aurora

A sounding rocket launched from Poker Flat
Research Range in Fairbanks, Alaska, Nov. 8, 2023,
carrying the DISSIPATION mission. The rocket
launched into aurora and successfully captured data
to understand how auroras heat the atmosphere and
cause high-altitude winds.
